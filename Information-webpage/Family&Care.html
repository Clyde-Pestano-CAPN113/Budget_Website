Family & Care

Family and care refers to the financial and emotional support systems that individuals provide for their family members, encompassing both direct care responsibilities and broader familial relationships. This concept includes several key aspects:

  • Childcare
  • Elder Care
  • Health and Wellness
  • Emotional Support
  • Family Activities

Childcare

This involves the responsibilities and expenses associated with raising children, including daycare, education, and extracurricular activities that support their development and well-being.

Elder Care

This pertains to the care and support provided to aging family members, which may include financial assistance, health care services, and daily living support to ensure their comfort and safety.

Health and Wellness

Family and care also encompass the health-related expenses and services that families invest in to maintain the physical and mental well-being of all members, including health insurance, medical care, and wellness programs.

Accessing Mental Health Care at Humber College

The Canada Health Act helps ensure that all eligible residents of Canada have reasonable access to insured health services, without financial barriers. No Canadian should have to pay for medically necessary health care services, and the federal government is continuously working to uphold this principle for every Canadian.

As part of the federal government's work to deliver better health outcomes for Canadians, it has now signed agreements with all provinces and territories to begin delivering the $25 billion in new funding available in the ten-year health care plan through bilateral agreements.

Family Activities

Engaging in shared experiences, such as family outings, vacations, and celebrations, is also part of family and care, as these activities strengthen bonds and create lasting memories.

Vacations: Opportunities for exploration and adventure, which can enhance learning and appreciation for different cultures.

Family Outings: For example, Zoo or Aquarium Visits, Educational and entertaining, these outings can spark curiosity in children.

Celebrations: Marking special occasions helps families come together and celebrate milestones.
