What is Investing?

Investing is the process of allocating money with the expectation of achieving a profit or financial gain over time. Smart investing can help you grow your wealth and secure your financial future.

  • Start early to benefit from compounding.
  • Diversify your portfolio to reduce risks.
  • Understand your financial goals and risk tolerance.
